What is Xeriscaping?

Xeriscaping is a landscaping technique that involves designing gardens or outdoor spaces that require little to no water beyond what is naturally provided by rainfall. This method prioritizes using native plants that are adapted to the local climate and soil conditions, requiring less maintenance and resources. By incorporating plants that are drought-resistant, xeriscaping reduces the need for frequent watering, resulting in significant water savings. Xeriscaping also emphasizes soil improvement and the use of organic mulches, which help retain moisture in the soil, preventing evaporation and maintaining healthy plant growth. Overall, xeriscaping is a sustainable and eco-friendly landscaping method that helps conserve water and reduce environmental impact while still allowing for beautiful and functional outdoor spaces.
