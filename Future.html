Our Future

We remain fully dedicated to our project, persistently working towards the goal of developing a user-friendly app that caters to a diverse audience. Our focus lies in expanding our database and outreach to various regions and communities, allowing the integration of Xeriscaping to flourish. To enrich the user experience, we aspire to incorporate augmented reality features, enabling users to visualize plants in their homes or gardens in real-time. Our ultimate objective is to disseminate Xeriscaping knowledge globally, thereby making a valuable contribution to the fight against climate change.
